Aquarius Lemon whip

INVENTIVE AND INDEPENDENT

aprox. 40 min
7 Servings


60 buttery round crackers, crushed
1/4 C confectioners' sugar
1/2 C melted butter
6 oz frozen lemonade concentrate
14 oz sweetend condensed milk
12 oz whipped topping thawed


1. In a mixing bowl, combine crushed crackes, confectioners' sugar and melted butter. Press into bottom of 9x13 in. pan; reserve 1/3 cup for sprinkling on top.

2. Blend together lemonade, sweetend condensed milk and whipped topping. Pour over crust and sprinkle withe remaining crumb mixture. If desired, garnish with thickly sliced lemons.
